Best Pesto Pasta with Salmon Recipe

Home

Description

Delicious Pesto Pasta with alot of Parmigiano Reggiano and perfectly cooked Salmon inside an Airfryer on the side.

After cooking it once you will definetly want to cook it a second time (guaranteed).

Ingredients for the Pesto Pasta

  • around 40 leafs of fresh Basil
  • 45 grams of Cashew nuts (raw and unsalted)
  • 45 grams of freshly grated Parmigiano Reggiano
  • 35 grams of high quality olive oil
  • 2 small cloves of garlic
  • Pasta water of Pasta that is almost finished cooking
  • Salt and Pepper

Ingredients for the Salmon

  • 300 grams of Salmon fillet
  • 1-2 teaspoons of Olive Oil
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic
  • Some Thyme
  • Some smoked paprika
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

Before anything heat up the airfryer to 200 째C or 400째F and roast the raw and unsalted Cashew nuts for around 4-5 minutes. (check so you don't burn them)

Prepare the Salmon

  1. Heat up the Airfyer to 200 째C or 400 째F.
  2. Add olive oil, thyme, smoked paprika, salt and pepper into a bowl. Crush the garlic cloves with a garlic press or grate the garlic and add them too. Mix everything.
  3. Put the Salmon on the Crisper Plate inside the Airfyer and brush the whole mix inside the bowl onto it.
  4. Set the Airfryer to 13 minutes and let it cook for that duration. Meanwhile prepare the Pesto Pasta.

Prepare the Pesto Pasta

  1. Wash the Basil leafs.
  2. Crush the Garlic cloves or grate them.
  3. Add everything from the Pesto Pasta ingredients list into a small mixer and let it mix until smooth.
  4. Follow the cooking steps on the packaging of the pasta of your choice (Penne or Spaghetti are my favourite choices).
  5. Right before the Pasta finishes to cook take out around 100-200ml of Pasta water (depends how creamy you want it) and add it to the mixer with the Pesto inside.
  6. Let the Mixer mix the Pesto again for around a minute and add the Pesto to the Pasta (that you have drained) and mix.

Put the Pasta and the Salmon on a plate and grate more Parmegiano Reggiano on top of the pasta.
