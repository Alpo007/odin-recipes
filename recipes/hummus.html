Description

Self made Hummus that isn't just easy to make but also tastes really good. You could add any toppings you like. For example Tuna, ground beef or only olive oil.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of chickpeas (220g)
  • 2 tablespoons of Tahini
  • juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 tablespoon of Cumin powder
  • 5 tablespoons of cold water
  • Salt and Pepper
