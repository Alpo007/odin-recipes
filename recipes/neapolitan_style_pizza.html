Neapolitan Style Pizza

Home

Description

Soft and crunchy Neapolitan Style Pizza made with 24h rested Poolish, fresh Mozzarella and San Marzano Tomatoes.

The Pizza toppings can be chosen based on personal liking.

Prepare the Poolish 16-24h before making the Pizza

  • 150 grams of Water
  • 150 grams of Pizza flour
  • 3 grams of yeast
  • 3 grams of honey

Steps for the Poolish

  1. Add everything into a bowl and mix together.
  2. Cover the bowl with foil and poke 3 tiny holes into it.
  3. Let it rest for 1h at room temperature.
  4. Let it rest in your fridge for 16-24h.
